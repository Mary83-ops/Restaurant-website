    A sizzling plate of juicy tandoori chicken pieces served with fragrant basmati rice, butter naan, and a side of cool cucumber raita.

    Tandoori chicken is a popular Indian dish that's marinated in yogurt and spices, then roasted in a clay oven. Our version is cooked to perfection and served on a sizzling platter that's sure to impress. Pair it with our fluffy basmati rice and warm butter naan for a complete meal that's packed with flavor.

    A flavorful bowl of spiced basmati rice mixed with fresh veggies, topped with crispy fried onions and served with a side of tangy tamarind chutney.

    Biryani is a classic Indian dish that's made with fragrant basmati rice and a variety of spices, veggies, and sometimes meat. Our veggie biryani is a vegetarian version that's just as delicious, with a medley of fresh veggies cooked to perfection and mixed with aromatic spices. Top it off with some crispy fried onions and a drizzle of tangy tamarind chutney for a burst of flavor in every bite.

    Tender chicken pieces marinated in a spiced yogurt sauce, served over a bed of fluffy basmati rice and topped with a creamy tomato-based curry sauce.

    Chicken tikka masala is a beloved Indian dish that's known for its creamy tomato-based sauce and tender chunks of chicken. Our version features juicy chicken pieces that are marinated in a yogurt sauce with a blend of spices, then grilled to perfection. We serve it over a bed of fluffy basmati rice and top it with a creamy tomato-based curry sauce that's sure to satisfy your cravings.

    Tender pieces of lamb cooked in a spicy vindaloo sauce, served with warm garlic naan and a side of cool cucumber raita.

    Vindaloo is a fiery Indian curry that's known for its intense spice and tangy flavor. Our lamb vindaloo is a classic version that features tender pieces of lamb cooked in a spicy sauce with a blend of spices and vinegar. We serve it with warm garlic naan to soak up all the delicious sauce, and a side of cool cucumber raita to balance out the heat.

    A hearty bowl of chickpeas cooked in a spiced tomato-based sauce, served over a bed of fragrant basmati rice and topped with crispy fried onions.

    Chana masala is a vegetarian dish that's made with chickpeas and a variety of spices, cooked in a tomato-based sauce. Our version features a hearty bowl of chickpeas cooked to perfection in a flavorful sauce that's packed with aromatic spices. We serve it over a bed of fragrant basmati rice and top it with crispy fried onions for added texture and flavor.
